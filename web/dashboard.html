Principles to Hold

1
Domain depth beats role breadth
The ME-PM who knows digital twins and supply chains cold beats the generalist who's touched five industries. Optionality comes from depth.
2
Never anchor the salary low
Your first offer sets the floor for a decade. Defend $200K+ TC, aim for $300K+. Every $10K of base shortens the FIRE path ~4–6 months.
3
Every course answers an interview question
ME 276DS → "can you reason about ML?" ME 235 → "have you built hardware?" MBA 267 → "do you think like a PM?" Nothing on the transcript is for breadth alone.
4
Build in public
Keep shipping side projects. Your GitHub and portfolio site are recruiting assets classmates don't have. Don't let them go dark at Berkeley.
5
Ship in your first 90 days
In every role, be known for shipping something real, fast. That reputation compounds faster than any title or credential.
6
WLB is designed, not granted
Protect surf sessions, long runs, and travel like board meetings. Work expands to fill whatever time you give it.

The Custom Concentration

4 courses · 12 units · petition-built
The ME MEng concentration requires 12 units of 200-level coursework. The standard tracks (Advanced Energy, Aerospace, Biomechanics, Control of Robotic & Autonomous Systems, Fluids, MEMS/Nano, Mechanics & Dynamics, Modeling & Simulation of Advanced Manufacturing, Product Design) are physical-science tracks — none maps cleanly to a commercial PM target list. Per the Academic Guide, "Course substitutions may be allowed with the consent of the department ME MEng faculty advisor." That's the door to a custom concentration spanning hardware, ML, digital twins, and product development.
3u
ME 235
Microprocessor-Based Mechanical Systems
Embedded hardware, sensors, thermal components, instruments. Serves Samsara (IoT fleet), Rivian, Figure AI, Zipline. Prereq: ME 132 or undergrad controls equivalent.
4u
ME 276DS
Statistics & Data Science for Engineers
Probability, optimization, ML estimation, supervised learning, decision trees, deep learning, CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, time series, clustering, PCA. Python-based. Serves Databricks, NVIDIA, Scale AI.
3u
ME 239
Modeling, Simulation & Digital Twins
Digital twins is literally Samsara's product category. Flexport uses them for shipment visibility; Microsoft D365 SCM runs on twin architecture; Rivian for vehicle lifecycle.
3u
ME 290P
New Product Development
Core PM methodology delivered through the ME department. Rounds the concentration to exactly 12u — or yields to IEOR 221 if the petition is approved as a 5th course (13u).
IEOR 221 petition strategy. Introduction to Financial Engineering is the finance anchor (interest rates, arbitrage, Brownian motion, options pricing, Black-Scholes) for any fintech conversation. Best: petition approved, counts as a 5th concentration course. Acceptable: denied, take it supplemental and non-degree-counting. Fallback: skip it, rely on MBA 203 + 231, add another ME Product Design course. Confirm with your ME faculty advisor before Fall 2026 orientation.
